7th Legion

The 7th Legion was a legion of the Grand Army of the Republic under the command of Senior Clone Commander CT-80/88-3009 A'den during the Clone Wars. The 7th Legion was part of the 327th Star Corps, led by Clone Marshal Commander CC-5052 Bly and Jedi General Aayla Secura. The 101st Regiment was part of the 7th Legion, as was the 3rd Brigade.
