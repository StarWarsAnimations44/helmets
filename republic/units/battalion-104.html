104th Battalion

The 104th Battalion, also known as the Wolf Pack Battalion, or the Wolfpack Battalion, was a clone trooper military battalion of the Grand Army of the Republic. The 104th was led by Jedi General Plo Koon and Clone Commander CC-3636, also known by his clone trooper nickname of Wolffe, throughout the Clone Wars. A subunit of the battalion was the similarly nicknamed Wolfpack squad.
