Clones

Categorie Detail Phase I Phase II
Commander

Clone Commanders were highly skilled leaders of the clone troopers during the Clone Wars. They were trained to lead small squads, platoons, and sometimes even entire battalions of clone troopers.

Captain

Clone Captains were highly trained officers in charge of leading smaller units of clone troopers, typically consisting of around 60 to 100 soldiers.

Lieutenant

Clone Lieutenants were officers in the Grand Army of the Republic who held a rank below that of Clone Captains. They typically led squads of about ten clone troopers and served as the link between the Clone Captain and the individual soldiers under their command.

Sergeant

Able to command squads, this is the lowest rank in the GAR. They can also be used to subordinate a superior to lead larger units.

Trooper

Here is the cannon fodder of the republic. These clone troopers raised on Kamino number around 3,000,000. The very heart of the GAR, they were the key to victory on Geonosis. Fortunately, these basic clones will then be able to specialize throughout the war.

Para

Among the many special corps that emerged, that of the Para Infantryman stood out from the others for its unique ability to enter a battlefield from high altitudes. Their specialty lay in combat insertion from the air.

Advanced Recon Force Scout

Advanced Recon Force Scout Troopers, also referred to as Advanced Recon Force troopers, ARF troopers, or as AT-RT Drivers, were clone troopers who wore lighter armor for stealth and speed and often rode All Terrain Reconnaissance Transports into battle. They were more intelligent and better trained in survival skills in comparison to other clones.

Advanced Recon Commando

Advanced Recon Commandos, also known as ARC troopers, were an elite variant of the clone troopers that served the Galactic Republic. Chosen from the ranks of the Grand Army of the Republic, the status of ARC trooper was bestowed on clones who served with distinction on the battlefield, such as CT-1409 Echo and CT-5555 Fives.

Biker Advanced Recon Commando

Biker Advanced Recon Commandos, also referred to as BARC troopers, were special forces clone troopers of the Grand Army of the Republic that were trained to ride BARC speeders across difficult terrain or dangerous battlegrounds.

Cold Assault

Clone cold assault troopers, also known as cold assault clone troopers, were a specialized variant of clone troopers within the Grand Army of the Republic during the Clone Wars. They were equipped with HT-77 Cold Assault Armor and other cold-assault gear to handle combat in frigid environments.

Pilot

Clone trooper pilots, also known as clone pilots, were clone troopers that, due to having particularly good reflexes and spatial awareness, were spotted early and brought into pilot training.

Engineers

Clone Engineers were clone trooper engineers of the Galactic Republic that were active during the Clone Wars against the Confederacy of Independent Systems. Clone Engineers made sure that the Republic's vehicles were in top condition, and were equipped with specialized Phase I clone trooper armor and later Phase II clone trooper armor.

Scout

Like some of their colleagues, the clone scouts underwent special training, in this case centered on the mastery of their favorite vehicles. Clone Army scouts wore a slightly modified version of Phase II armor.
